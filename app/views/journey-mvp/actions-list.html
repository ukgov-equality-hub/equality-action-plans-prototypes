Create your action plan

Below is the list of actions you can choose from. For each action, we will ask if your organisation:

  • is already doing it as part of its working culture
  • is doing it but not routinely
  • wants to include it in your action plan – you plan to start doing it in the next year
  • does not want to include it in your action plan

You must add a status for every action. You can also add a supporting narrative for each action to explain what your organisation is doing or plans to do in this area.

Your plan must include actions with which you can show further progress. For example, if your organisation is updating its workplace flexibility practices then this can be included. Actions already fully embedded within your organisation and not currently being progressed do not count for this purpose.

Your action plan must include:

  • at least one action from the ‘Supporting staff during menopause’ category
  • at least one action from any other category, for example ‘Creating a more flexible workplace’

You will be able to review and edit your full action plan before you submit it.

In the real service users will have to assess every action in every category. To save time for those using the prototype, this is not enforced here! Actions or categories you skip will be treated as “not pursuing currently”.
