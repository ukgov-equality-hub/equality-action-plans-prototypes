Equality action plans

From April 2027, organisations with over 250 employees must submit an action plan with their gender pay gap report. This plan must show how you will:

  • reduce your gender pay gap
  • improve gender equality in your organisation
Read more about creating an equality action plan on GOV.UK.

Before you start

You should make sure you:

  • know which actions you will be selecting
  • have prepared your supporting text

You need to select at least 2 new actions in your first action plan.

You can also add:

  • supporting text for each action – this should explain what your organisation is doing or plans to do in this area
  • a supporting narrative for your overall action plan – this should give any background information including your organisation’s objectives for equality, diversity and inclusion
