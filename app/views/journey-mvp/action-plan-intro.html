Gender pay gap action plans

From 2027, organisations with over 250 employees must submit an action plan with their gender pay gap report. This plan must show how you will:

  • address your gender pay gap
  • support employees going through the menopause

You must choose from our list of actions. These are backed by evidence which demonstrates their effectiveness. The actions cover the following categories:

  • analysing your data
  • recruiting staff
  • building diversity into your organisation
  • promoting and retaining staff
  • supporting staff during menopause
  • creating a more flexible workplace

In future years, you will review your existing plan rather than creating a brand new one.

This service will guide you through the process of choosing actions for your plan.
